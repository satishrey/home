Cristiano Ronaldo & Lionel Messi

Who Are They?

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional soccer player who currently plays in the Spanish league (Liga BBVA) for Real Madrid as a forward. He is arguably the best soccer player right now. According to Forbes 2017 report, he is the highest paid athlete in the world. On the other hand, Lionel Messi is an Argentinian professional soccer player who currently plays in the Spanish league for F.C. Barcelona as a forward. Like Ronaldo, Messi is arguably the best soccer player right now. He is the third highest paid athlete in the world.

Why Compare Them?

Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi are some of the biggest and most talked about athletes in the sports industry. The clubs that they play for have one of the biggest rivalries in the sports industry as well. Because they are both such amazing players, soccer fans love to pick sides and compare the two players. Ronaldo and Messi have both said in interviews that they are not rivals, which may be true, but both players would definitely prefer to outshine the other.
